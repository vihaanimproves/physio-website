Our Services

Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services in Ghatkopar West

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or looking to improve your physical performance, we offer specialized treatment tailored to your needs.

Orthopedic Manual Therapy

Advanced hands-on techniques to restore joint mobility, improve movement, and relieve musculoskeletal pain.

  • Neck pain, back pain, shoulder injuries
  • Knee pain, post-fracture rehab
  • Arthritis management

Dry Needling Therapy

Precision needle therapy targeting muscle trigger points to release tension, reduce pain, and accelerate healing.

  • Chronic muscle pain
  • Sports injuries
  • Tension headaches, myofascial pain

Neurodynamic Solutions

Specialized treatment for nerve-related pain and mobility restrictions using evidence-based neurodynamic techniques.

  • Sciatica, carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Nerve impingement
  • Radiating pain, numbness, tingling

Sports Physiotherapy

Comprehensive assessment and treatment for athletes and active individuals — from injury recovery to performance optimization.

  • Runners, gym-goers, athletes
  • Sports injury recovery
  • Performance enhancement

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured recovery programs following surgery to restore function, rebuild strength, and return you to daily activities safely.

  • ACL reconstruction, knee replacement
  • Shoulder surgery recovery
  • Spinal surgery rehabilitation

Kinesio Taping

Therapeutic taping techniques that support injured muscles and joints while maintaining full range of motion.

  • Sports injury recovery
  • Postural support
  • Lymphatic drainage, injury prevention
