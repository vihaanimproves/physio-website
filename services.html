Sports Physiotherapy

Get back in the game with specialized treatment for athletic injuries and performance optimization.

What Is Sports Physiotherapy?

Sports physiotherapy focuses on acute injury, on-field physiotherapy, full-body assessment, and treating injuries related to sports. Whether you are a professional athlete or a weekend warrior, this specialized care helps you recover faster and return to your sport.

Conditions We Treat

  • ACL and other ligament injuries
  • Muscle strains and tears
  • Tennis elbow and golfer's elbow
  • Shoulder injuries (rotator cuff, dislocations)
  • Ankle sprains and instability
  • Overuse injuries and tendinitis
  • Running-related injuries

Our Approach

We begin with a comprehensive assessment of your injury, movement patterns, and sport-specific demands. Treatment combines manual therapy, targeted exercises, and functional training to not only heal your injury but also prevent future ones. We focus on:

  • Pain reduction and inflammation management
  • Restoring full range of motion
  • Rebuilding strength and endurance
  • Sport-specific conditioning
  • Injury prevention strategies

Expected Timeline

Recovery varies based on injury severity. Minor sprains may heal in 2-4 weeks, while complex ligament injuries may require 3-6 months of rehabilitation. We'll give you realistic expectations from day one.

Start Your Recovery

Who Is This For?

  • Athletes of all levels
  • Weekend sports enthusiasts
  • Runners and cyclists
  • Anyone with sports injuries

Techniques Used

  • Manual therapy
  • Therapeutic exercise
  • Taping and bracing
  • Functional training
  • Biomechanical analysis

Orthopedic Pain Management

Comprehensive care for bone, joint, and muscle conditions to restore function and eliminate pain.

What Is Orthopedic Manual Therapy?

Orthopedic physiotherapy addresses musculoskeletal conditions affecting bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. It's ideal for recovering from fractures, managing arthritis, or treating chronic joint problems.

Conditions We Treat

  • Arthritis (osteoarthritis, rheumatoid)
  • Post-fracture rehabilitation
  • Joint replacements (hip, knee)
  • Chronic back and neck pain
  • Degenerative disc disease
  • Bursitis and tendinitis
  • Repetitive strain injuries

Treatment Process

We start with a detailed evaluation of your joint function, muscle strength, and movement patterns. Your personalized treatment plan may include:

  • Joint mobilization techniques
  • Strengthening exercises
  • Pain management strategies
  • Posture correction
  • Home exercise programs

What to Expect

Most patients notice improvement within 4-6 sessions. Chronic conditions may require ongoing maintenance care. We'll work together to manage your symptoms and improve your quality of life.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Post-surgical physiotherapy focuses on everything related to recovery phase post-operation, and also follows the same approach part.

Why Post-Surgical Physiotherapy?

Surgery is just the beginning of your recovery. Physiotherapy after surgery is crucial for regaining strength, mobility, and function. It helps prevent complications, reduces pain, and speeds up your return to normal activities.

Surgeries We Support

  • Joint replacement (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • ACL reconstruction
  • Rotator cuff repair
  • Spinal surgery
  • Fracture fixation
  • Arthroscopic procedures
  • Abdominal and thoracic surgeries

Dry Needling Therapy (CDNP)

Precision needle therapy targeting muscle trigger points to release tension, reduce pain, and accelerate healing.

What Is Dry Needling?

Dry needling is a modern treatment technique where fine needles are inserted into trigger points in muscles to release tension and reduce pain. It's highly effective for chronic muscle pain and sports injuries.

Conditions We Treat

  • Chronic muscle pain and stiffness
  • Tension headaches and migraines
  • Sports injuries and muscle strains
  • Myofascial pain syndrome
  • Neck and shoulder tension
  • Lower back pain

What to Expect

Most patients notice improvement within 2-4 sessions. The treatment may cause temporary soreness, but this typically resolves within 24-48 hours. We'll guide you through the entire process and adjust based on your response.

Benefits

  • Fast pain relief
  • Improved flexibility
  • Reduced muscle tension
  • Enhanced recovery

Ergonomic and Posture Correction

Optimizing how your body interacts with your environment to prevent strain and injury.

What Is It About?

Ergonomics and Posture Correction focus on optimizing how your body interacts with your environment to prevent unnecessary strain and injury. Whether you work at a desk or are active all day, poor alignment can unknowingly lead to chronic pain, fatigue, and long-term musculoskeletal issues. Our program analyzes your daily habits and workspace to correct these imbalances, helping you move efficiently and live pain-free.

Conditions We Treat

  • Tech Neck / Text Neck

  • Chronic Lower Back Pain
  • Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI)

  • Scoliosis Management

Treatment Approach
