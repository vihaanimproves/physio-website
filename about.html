Meet Dr. Yashvi Shah

Your Partner in Movement, Recovery & Pain-Free Living

BPTh Qualified 4+ Years Experience

Dedicated to Your Complete Recovery

Movement is the foundation of a life well-lived. I am Dr. Yashvi Shah (PT), a specialized Musculoskeletal Physiotherapist and the founder of HealActive Physiotherapy Clinic. With over four years of dedicated clinical experience, my mission is to bridge the gap between persistent pain and peak physical performance.

My clinical approach is built on a foundation of academic excellence and continuous specialized training. After earning my Bachelor of Physiotherapy (B.P.Th) from the prestigious K.J. Somaiya College of Physiotherapy, I pursued advanced certifications to provide my patients with a comprehensive toolkit for recovery, including:

  • Orthopedic Manual Therapy (COMT)
  • Dry Needling (CDNP) & Kinesio Taping (CKTP)
  • Neurodynamic Solutions (NDS)

At HealActive, I believe that "one-size-fits-all" has no place in healthcare. I am committed to evidence-based practice, where I combine clinical expertise with your specific lifestyle needs to design a customized recovery protocol. Whether you are navigating a sports injury, chronic pain, or post-surgical rehabilitation, my goal is to provide more than just treatment — I provide a partnership in your journey toward optimal mobility and long-term wellness.

Why I Became a Physiotherapist

I chose to become a physiotherapist because I am deeply fascinated by the human body's resilience and the profound impact movement has on our daily lives. Watching someone lose their independence to pain or injury is difficult, but guiding them back to strength is incredibly rewarding. I specialized in musculoskeletal care and founded HealActive to create a space where evidence-based science meets genuine empathy. For me, physiotherapy isn't just about treating a symptom; it's about designing personalized paths to help people regain their mobility, overcome pain, and confidently return to the lives they love.

My Treatment Philosophy

Patient-Centered Care

You're not just a patient number. I take the time to understand your unique story, goals, and concerns. Your treatment plan is built around your life, not a textbook protocol.

Evidence Meets Empathy

I combine the latest research and proven techniques with genuine compassion. Science guides my methods, but empathy guides my approach to your care.

Education & Empowerment

Knowledge is healing. I believe in teaching you about your condition so you can make informed decisions and take an active role in your recovery.

Holistic Approach

Pain doesn't exist in isolation. I consider how your condition affects your work, sleep, mood, and daily activities, addressing the whole picture of your health.

Realistic Expectations

I'm honest about timelines and outcomes. No false promises — just realistic goals, clear communication, and consistent support throughout your journey.

Long-Term Wellness

My goal isn't just to treat your current problem — it's to equip you with the knowledge and habits to prevent future injuries and maintain optimal health.
